Gani Fawehinmi Park - Ojota, Lagos-Nigeria

My travel tour to great Gani Fawehinmi park in Ojota.

The statue in the park is dedicate to an international activist of repute and defender of rights and justice.

This is a symbolic park named after the iconic legend, Chief Gani Fawehinmi. A lawyer per excellence and an advocate for the common man.
Today, the park service is serving as a melting pot for all struggles emanating in the mega city (Lagos). In 2010, a park in Ojota was named after him by the Lagos State Government
